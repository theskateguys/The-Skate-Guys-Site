Quick Answers

FAQ

Is it beginner friendly?

Absolutely. We teach from first stand‑up to advanced dance moves.

Do you accept all ages?

Yes—kids, teens, adults, and family groups. We place by comfort level.

Do I need my own skates?

Bring Your Own if you have; otherwise request rentals and add your shoe size.

How do I pay?

We’ll confirm your spot and share secure payment options in WhatsApp.

Are classes crowded?

No—spots are capped to keep floors safe and coaching personal.

Can I try before joining?

Yes—ask for our next free Community Day or book a single class.
